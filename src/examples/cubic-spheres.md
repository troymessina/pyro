# Cubic Arrangement of Spheres

A introduction to VPython's 3D primitives. This example creates a static scene with eight spheres of different colors, each positioned on the corner of a cube.

## What You'll See

- **Spheres** — Eight spheres arranged on a cubic lattice. The spheres have a radius of 0.5 and are spaced on a cube of dimension 1.

## Concepts Demonstrated

- `sphere(pos, radius, color)` — Position, size, and color

## Tips

- Drag to rotate the view and explore the scene from different angles
- Use scroll to zoom in and out
- Great starting point for understanding VPython's coordinate system and shape API
